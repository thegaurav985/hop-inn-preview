Our Story

Small hostel.
Big Tapovan energy.

We started Hop Inn because we kept ending up in the same place — that worn wooden bench by the Ganga at dusk, chai in hand, talking to strangers who became friends.

Tapovan is the quieter, cooler side of Rishikesh. No package tour crowds. Just backpackers, yogis, cafe-hoppers, and people who found this place by accident and stayed too long. We made a hostel that fits all of them.

Prime Tapovan Location 500m from Ganga Ghats. Quiet lanes, zero tourist hustle.
Real Community Rooftop hangouts, group treks, cooking nights.
Flexible Check-in Late bus? Early flight? We work with your schedule.
